Our Mission

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Alert X began with a simple idea: security systems should be smarter, more responsive, and accessible to everyone. Our founders, a team of AI researchers and security experts, recognized that traditional surveillance systems were failing to keep pace with modern threats.

What started as a small research project has grown into a comprehensive security platform trusted by businesses, governments, and individuals worldwide. Our journey has been defined by innovation, perseverance, and a commitment to excellence.
